Core Values

These ‘core values’ are the values on which Eclipse Systems company culture is based.

The best companies develop core values that impact their company culture, brand, and business strategies — making them quite unique. So we took an inside look at the core values of highly-engaged and successful companies to learn what makes them tick, what they believe and how they do business.

Here are 10 core values of 15 high-performance companies–across various industries–that aim to deliver more than profit with a values-based business approach:

1. Deliver WOW Through Service
2. Embrace and Drive Change
3. Create Fun and A Little Weirdness
4. Be Adventurous, Creative, and Open-Minded
5. Pursue Growth and Learning
6. Build Open and Honest Relationships With Communication
7. Build a Positive Team and Family Spirit
8. Do More With Less
9. Be Passionate and Determined
10. Be Humble
