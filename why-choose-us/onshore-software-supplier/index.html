Onshore Software Supplier

Eclipse Systems provides an onshore software development outsourcing service for UK customers. However, offshore software development outsourcing has become popular in recent years, initially to countries such as India and more recently to Eastern Europe and the Far East. These countries offer cheaper daily rates than the UK and the prospect of a much reduced total development cost. But should you go offshore with your project?

Specialists in onshore software development and digital technology

Britain has all the talent, skill and knowledge needed to provide high quality, cost-effective solutions to the software outsourcing requirements of any UK business.

The Eclipse Systems software development philosophy

Britain has all the talent, skill and knowledge needed to provide high quality, cost-effective solutions to the software outsourcing requirements of any UK business.

It is our objective to deliver these solutions by recruiting the brightest and most highly motivated school-leavers and graduates for our teams.

Working under the expert guidance and scrutiny of established specialists, these talented teams are the solutions engine room that enables us to deliver high-performing outsourcing at a price to rival offshore suppliers.
