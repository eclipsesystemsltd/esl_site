Software Support

Eclipse Systems provides a first class support service, both for systems that we develop and for those developed by third parties.

Reliable support of third party software and systems

Has your business critical system become difficult to support in-house? Whether your original developers are no longer available or your system is so heavily customised that it’s become unstable, at Eclipse Systems, our developers are experts in the support of unfamiliar, third party systems.

With comprehensive technology and programming knowledge, our team can help extend the life of your systems and minimise the risk of downtime. Our system support services will:

  • Provide responsive support for emergencies or scheduled maintenance
  • Reduce long term costs by improving the quality of your code
  • Mitigate business risk by stabilising business critical systems
  • Identify inefficiencies and recommend improvements
  • Enable the integration of other systems, applications and mobile

Why choose Eclipse Systems for your third party software support?

Emphasis on quality

At Eclipse Systems we don’t just fix bugs in the system. Our analysts and software engineers work with you to understand your business requirements and technology goals. And we do it historically, as well as looking to future proof your software. If the original documentation is not available, our skilled team will thoroughly audit to identify what has been done in the past and make recommendations to minimise risk and improve overall quality.

Tailor-made support

As a bespoke software house, we know that every business is different, with wide ranging IT needs. That’s why we tailor-make all our support agreements. We’ll work with you to consider whether your business requires onsite resource, flexible pricing or 24/7 response times, to ensure you get the right level of support for your business critical systems.

Direct software developer access

Businesses today often operate around the clock according to their customers’ needs. We understand that system downtime, impacts the bottom line and in an emergency, you need to contact the right person, quickly, and at any time. At Eclipse Systems we don’t hide our developers behind account managers. If you have a support contract with us, we’ll give you direct access to the people who can fix your system, when you need them.
