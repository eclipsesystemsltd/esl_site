Business Analysis

A correctly implemented software solution can bring vast benefits to any business.

Eclipse Systems can help you make the right choice and avoid problematical solutions.

We provide expert business analysis to help you choose wisely. Our analysts have seen many systems both good and bad and can pass that knowlege on to you.

Typical problems we can solve:

  • Your existing systems are not aligned to your business needs and you want to improve them.
  • You want independant external technical advice to ensure you have an effective software strategy.
  • You want external help, able to solve a particular business problem in the context of your wider organisation.
  • You have a vaguely-defined business requirement and need more detailed analysis to realise it.
  • We make sure you’re embarking upon the appropriate project for your business in the best way. We’ve worked for organisations of all sizes, from small start-ups to large corporations, and delivered high value to them all.

Whatever you need, whatever your business, we’ll help you utilise your software better.

  • We’ll review your situation and discuss your objectives.
  • We’ll provide dedicated business analysis to get a good understanding of your business.
  • We’ll assist you with your requirements-gathering – talking to key staff throughout your organisation to identify possible improvements through software.
  • We’ll review what existing software is available, both within your organisation and via third parties, to ensure the best value for money. We’ll provide comprehensive analysis covering what you have, what you need and how to get there.

Find out how our analysts can help you.
